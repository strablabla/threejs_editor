3D scene editor — physics sandbox

Create objects, animate them with a physics engine (gravity, elastic collisions, springs), and save your scenes as JSON on the server.

Create — pick a tool in the Object panel (clickable icons) or with the keyboard (see the Keys tab), then click in the plane. A box is drawn in 2 clicks.

Animate — a start, x play / pause.

Physics — the Parameters panel controls gravity, springs, the 1/r² interaction (with its strength / softening), Fast collisions (cell lists) and Fast attraction (Barnes–Hut).

Monitoring (Parameters → Monitoring): energy graph, velocity histogram, altitude histogram (with a z observation window + bins slider), and the trajectories window (x–y, z(t), MSD, |v|(t) — zoom, pan, real-time follow).

Scenes — the Scene panel saves / loads / renames experiments and organises them in virtual folders (right-click a scene). The ↻ arrow in the navbar reloads the current scene without opening the panel.

View — drag with the mouse to rotate / zoom (TrackballControls).

Full documentation: see the project README.md.
